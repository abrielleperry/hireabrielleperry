About

I’m currently pursuing a Full Stack Development degree at Atlas School, where I’m gaining hands-on experience in web application development using various technologies and frameworks. I’m passionate about creating user-friendly, accessible, and visually appealing applications for diverse audiences. Recently, I completed the Front-End trimester with Honor Roll and am now honing my Back-End skills. Before my studies at Atlas, I worked as a Trainer and Installer at CaptionCall, where I combined my technical knowledge with strong customer service skills. In this role, I excelled at understanding customers' needs, simplifying complex technical concepts, and adapting to evolving technologies. Looking ahead, I’m excited to leverage my growing web development skill set alongside my 7 years of customer service and 4 years of technical experience. My goal is to collaborate with businesses to develop and improve websites that enhance performance, user experience, and functionality, while delivering tailored, innovative solutions to meet strategic objectives. By applying my ability to understand user needs and business goals, I aim to contribute to the overall success of projects and drive measurable results for clients.
